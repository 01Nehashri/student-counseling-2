    Features and services

    Dreampath offers a range of services to enable career-seekers with the requisite information and tools to make a career choice aligned with their abilities, interests and situations. Dreampath has a special focus on underprivileged and marginalised youth.

    • Ask an Expert

      Do you have a career related question? Are you confused about education options? Do you want to know more about a particular career?Post your career or education related query here. Our experts will provide their response on your email id. You can also look for an answer on our FAQ page.

    • Career Counselling

      On request, DreamPath undertakes one-on-one career counselling. There is always a pair of counsellors involved. We use a systematic approach of questionnaires and assessments, and multiple interviews with both student and parents / guardians. The assessments are simple and provide the necessary input to the career counsellors. Each counsellee is provided with a formal, detailed report at the end of the process, which covers our advice, suggestions and next steps.

    • Workshops

      Dreampath’s Career Tree program is a career guidance workshop that is informative, interactive and activity-driven. It is typically conducted on-campus, but in recent times we have also conducted a modified version fully online. The program introduces children to the different professions they can pursue post 10th 12th, the education options available for these and also the job-oriented (vocational) options for children not interested able to continue further studies.
